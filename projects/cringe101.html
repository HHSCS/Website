Build the cringiest Website using HTML!

Suggestions:
1. Use lots of bright colors! <font color="DeepPink"> Colored Text </font>
2. Use different sizes! <font size="8"> 8pt Text </font>
3. Combine tags! <font color="DeepPink" size="6"> 6pt and DeepPink! </font>
4. Use marquee!
ex: <marquee direction="right" scrollamount="15"> Moving right; Speed of 15 </marquee>

5. Use GIFs and Images!
